Biography

Against the backdrop of life challenges and doubts,
Ajibola seeks to explore the vast terrain of human
existence through the lens of a traveller traversing
the future, the present and the past and exploring
subjects such as the community, hope, obstacles,
victories, chaos, peace and the irony of being.
His works are influenced by architecture, geography,
travel, steampunk and dystopian ideas, highlighted
by his experimental use of paper, wood, waste cartons,
and electrical and mechanical wastes among others, paying
particular attention to texture and form.
Ajíbólá Eedris Adékàńmbí is an Ibadan-born Nigerian
emerging artist working primarily with paper. His works
are notably influenced by his relative background in
architecture as a draughtsman, he often drifts between
still life and abstract expression. Other influences on
his works include the theme of futility, geography,
technology and simple life forms. He lives and works
in Lagos, Nigeria.
